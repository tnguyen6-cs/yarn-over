Miss Bella (LittleFriends Collection)
Miss Bella (LittleFriends Collection)

$ 4.05

English pattern by DioneDesign. Includes step-by-step pictures.

After a longer time, I have prepared a collection of little dolls for you called LittleFriends.

They are little cute friends who will please many little ladies or babies. Each is unique and is sure to find a great friend.

Miss Bella is the fifth of them, she is sweet, clever, bright, and friendly. I crocheted her mainly for my two-year-old daughter, who I hope will be very happy with her. In the end, she became a really sweet and cute doll ... and I fell in love.

Detailed PDF instructions will guide you step by step through the production of the doll. The manual is written in real detail with more than 60 pictures.
Difficulty: suitable for beginners (however crochet basics needed)

What do you need:
- Sport or DK weight Yarn (with ca. 150m/50g (Polyacryl) or 120m/50g (Cotton)). You can use a worsted weight yarn as well, but then I recommend hook nr. F or G and bigger eyes. The doll will be slightly bigger.
-- < 50g beige
-- < 50g white
-- < 20g light pink
-- < 20g pink
-- < 20g brown
-- < piece of black
- Stuffing (Polyester-filling)
-2 Plastic eyes with security lock (12mm) or two black beads
-Crochet hook Nr. 3,5mm / E hook
-Wool needle

Stitches used: chain, slip stitch, magic ring, single crochet, increase, (invisible) decrease, half double crochet, double crochet, back loops only, front loops only, crochet into a ring
Measurement: The doll is approx. 24cm / 9.5" high
